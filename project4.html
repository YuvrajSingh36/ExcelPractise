Task 1

CONCAT

In D2 use CONCAT to join First Name (B2), a space, and Last Name (C2). Fill down.

Show solution steps

Task 2

TEXTJOIN

In E2 use TEXTJOIN to join columns F2:H2 separated by "; " ignoring empty cells.

Show solution steps
  1. =TEXTJOIN("; ",TRUE,F2:H2)

Task 3

LOWER

In I2 convert the email in column G to lowercase.

Show solution steps
  1. =LOWER(G2)

Task 4

COUNTA

In cell K1 show the count of non-empty cells in B2:B100.

Show solution steps
  1. =COUNTA(B2:B100)

Task 5

Named range

Define the named range StaffEmails for G2:G100.

Show solution steps

Hyperlinks
